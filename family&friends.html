Q. What is the process to book the Family & Friends Private Cinema?

To reserve the Family & Friends Private Cinema experience, get in touch with us via email on ksa.booking@maf.co.ae mentioning
  1. Your preferred location
  2. The date and time
  3. Your choice of the Gold Package or the VIP Seats package
  4. The number of people you would like to invite
  5. Your choice of movie


Q. What are the ticket prices for different experiences?
